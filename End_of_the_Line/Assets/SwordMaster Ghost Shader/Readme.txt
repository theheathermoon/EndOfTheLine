Thank you for downloading this asset.

This package provide the vertex and fragment ghost shader for your game.

The HologramShaderDemo.unity demonstrate the ghost shader effect.

Shader settings :

_MainTex - the main texture
_BumpTex - the normal map
_GhostColor - the color of the ghost effect
_Brightness - the brightness of the ghost effect
