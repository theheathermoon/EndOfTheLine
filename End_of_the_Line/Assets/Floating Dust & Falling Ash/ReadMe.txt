---------------------------
Floating Dust & Falling Ash
---------------------------

This package contains 6 Falling Ash effects + 3 Floating Dust effects ready to use in your projects. Just drag & drop a prefab into your scene then you will see the effect. 

Contains:
16 ash/dust shapes

6 Falling Ash effects:
Falling Ash - Light
Falling Ash - Medium
Falling Ash - Heavy
Falling Ash (dark) - Light
Falling Ash (dark) - Medium
Falling Ash (dark) - Heavy

3 Floating Dust effects:
Floating Dust - Light
Floating Dust - Medium
Floating Dust - Heavy

4 demo scenes
